Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, tender pasta, and melted cheese. Baked to perfection, it offers a comforting blend of flavors and textures, making it a favorite for family meals and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 400g canned tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp basil
  • 200ml béchamel sauce
  • 200g mozzarella, 50g Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce: Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, add ground beef, and cook until browned. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook the pasta: Boil lasagne sheets according to package instructions, then drain
  3. Assemble the layers: Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish, followed by lasagne sheets, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese.
  4. Bake: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and bake for 30-40 minutes until golden and bubbly
  5. Serve: Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
