American Goulash

Home

Description

American Goulash is a hearty, one-pot comfort dish made with ground beef, elbow macaroni, and a rich tomato-based sauce. Simple yet flavorful, this classic meal is perfect for busy weeknights and packed with savory goodness

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp oregano
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 500ml beef broth
  • 100g cheddar cheese, shredded (optional)
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the beef: Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned
  2. Make the sauce: Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Pour in beef broth and bring to a simmer.
  3. Add pasta: Stir in macaroni, cover, and cook until tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Finish: Stir in cheese if using, then let rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy
