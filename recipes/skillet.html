Skillet Chicked Pasta

Home

Description

Skillet Chicken Pasta is a quick and delicious one-pan meal featuring tender chicken, pasta, and a creamy, flavorful sauce. Perfect for busy nights, this dish is both comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 cups pasta (penne or rotini)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400ml chicken broth
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 cup canned tomatoes (optional)
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken: Sauté diced chicken in olive oil until golden. Remove and set aside
  2. Sauté aromatics: In the same pan, cook onion and garlic until soft
  3. Add liquids and pasta: Stir in chicken broth, cream, tomatoes (if using), and seasoning. Add pasta and simmer until tender.
  4. Combine and finish: Return chicken to the skillet, stir in Parmesan, and cook for a few minutes. Serve hot
